About Us


St Mary's Residental Care Home is located in a large attractive grounds, with stunning views over the Derwent Valley on the edge of Ebchester.


The care home forms part of St Mary's Convent, which belongs to the congregations of the Sisters of Charity of Our Lady Good and Perpetual Succour. Our Care team and Sisters provide care within the home. The Sisters have dedicated themselves to the care of the elderly, here at St Mary's since 1937.

St Mary's is registered with the Care Quality Commission to accommodate elderly residents. Our staff are fully trained in dementia care. All religions, cultures and genders are welcomed into the home.

If you would like to recieve a copy of our brochure please contact us and a member of the team will be in touch.
